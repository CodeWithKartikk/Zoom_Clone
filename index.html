About

Zoom is a American communication technology which provides video telephony and online chat services based on peer to peer technology. It is used for telecommunication, social relations, Education, corporate, etc. It is used millions of people now a days due to covid-19 pandemic. Zoom (clone) objective is to provide virtual communication between two individuals or group of people. It is a open source free video conferencing platform that was developed by zoom video communications in 2011.
